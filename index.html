About Me

Hi! I'm Derrick — an independent contractor based in Vancouver, working with both residential and commercial clients on renovations, repairs, and custom projects.

I believe every job should be approached with honest, clear communication and a focus on work that stands the test of time. I recognize that hiring a contractor is about trust as much as it is about the job, and I take that responsibility seriously. My goal is to make sure clients understand their options and feel confident in the decisions being made throughout the process.

Whether it’s a home renovation, small repair, or commercial space update, I’m open to discussing ideas, offering practical guidance, and helping find solutions that fit both project and budget.

I take pride in delivering reliable, well-executed work that I can confidently stand behind. Outside of work, I spend most of my time skiing or adventuring with my dog.
